STORY

Based in the UAE, I operate at the intersection of technical precision and creative exploration. My path isn't traditional, and that is my greatest strength.

With a background as a Legal Trainee at the Royal Academy of Police in Bahrain, I developed an obsession with structure and logic. I transitioned this mindset into the Real Estate sector as a Property Consultant, where I mastered the art of negotiation and high-stakes client liaison.

Today, I translate those diverse experiences into Digital Architecture. Whether I am building full-stack applications like Orbit or visualizing machine learning data, I view every line of code as a strategic decision.

When I'm not at the keyboard, I'm likely in the ring training as a Kickboxer or behind a lens capturing cinematic narratives through Photography. I don't just build websites; I design immersive experiences.
