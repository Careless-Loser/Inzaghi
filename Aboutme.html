ABOUT ME

My work is defined by the intersection of structure and creative logic. Before transitioning into technology, I built my foundation within the rigid frameworks of law as a Legal Trainee at the Royal Academy of Police. This experience instilled a permanent respect for the invisible systems that govern complex environments—a mindset I later carried into the high-stakes landscape of Dubai Real Estate. I view every project as an architecture of rules, designed to solve problems with precision.

Currently pursuing Creative Computing at Bath Spa University, I translate system-based thinking into digital reality. My practice spans from Python logic and cybersecurity to the tactile world of hardware integration. I don't just build interfaces; I engineer end-to-end experiences. Whether developing 3D visualizations or full-stack applications, I prioritize a detailed, logical approach where every UX decision is a calculated move toward clarity and performance.

Beyond the technical, my discipline is anchored in the persistence of training as a kickboxer and the observant eye of a photographer. I operate with a focus on long-term systems rather than shortcuts, building digital platforms that are as robust as they are immersive. I am driven by the search for a perfect equilibrium where sophisticated technology meets intentional design.
